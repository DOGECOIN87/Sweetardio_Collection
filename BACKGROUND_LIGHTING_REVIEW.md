## Collection Lighting Standard

Primary Light Source:
Upper Left (10:30–11:00)

Shadow Direction:
Lower Right

Highlights:
Upper Left surfaces

Ambient Fill:
Soft and subtle

Contrast:
Moderate

Temperature:
Neutral to slightly warm

Do NOT introduce dramatic cinematic lighting.
Do NOT introduce multiple competing light sources.
Do NOT introduce strong rim lights.
Do NOT introduce bloom effects.

## Collection Color Philosophy

Foreground assets carry the brand colors.

Palette:

Oxford Blue      #070F34
Zaffre           #0313A6
Dark Violet      #9201CB
Hollywood Cerise #F715AB
Fluorescent Cyan #34EDF3

Backgrounds exist to support these colors.

Backgrounds should generally be:
- less saturated
- less visually dominant
- less contrast-heavy

than foreground assets.
